1. netstat -h -- it will give a brief summary of possibilities.
++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
2. netstat -ae -- it will give a quick summary of all the information.
++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
3. netstat -i  -- it will give a summary by interface.
++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
4. netstat -ie -- it will give the same output as ifconfig
++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
5. netstat -ae --raw --tcp --udp -- it will show info about the state of raw, TCP and UDP sockets. 
+++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
6. netstat -ae --unix -- To obtain information about UNIX domain sockets  
+++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
7. netstat -as -- to gain global statistics
