++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
2. gcc -O0 -S -o test.s test.c -- to generate assembly output after compilation ,O0 will restrict any optimization.

3. man gcc -- to see the optimization options details.
++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
